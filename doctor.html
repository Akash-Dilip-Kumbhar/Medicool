Leadership & Vision

Meet the Visionaries Behind Medicool Hospital

Medicool Hospital was founded with a clear vision — to deliver advanced, ethical, and patient-centered healthcare to every individual. Dr. Barkha Kukreja and Dr. Varsha Kukreja share a common belief that healthcare is not just about treatment, but about trust, compassion, and long-term well-being. Their approach blends modern medical excellence with a human touch, ensuring every patient feels heard, respected, and cared for. With a strong focus on innovation, transparency, and personalized care, they have built Medicool Hospital into a center of excellence where healing begins with empathy and ends with lasting results.

  • At Medicool Hospital, our vision has always been to provide compassionate, patient-first healthcare. We are dedicated to ethical medical practices and bringing advanced technology to our community, ensuring that everyone receives the highest standard of care in a comfortable and healing environment.

    Dr. Barkha Kukreja
    Director

  • We believe that excellent healthcare goes beyond just treating symptoms; it's about nurturing the overall well-being of our patients. Our modern facilities and dedicated team are here to offer advanced, personalized treatments.

    Dr. Varsha Kukreja
    Director

Meet Our Directors

Dr. Barkha Kukreja

Consultant General & Advanced Laparoscopic Surgeon
Cosmetologist | Trichologist | Obesity Consultant

Dr. Barkha Kukreja is a highly skilled and experienced medical professional with expertise in General & Advanced Laparoscopic Surgery, Cosmetology, Trichology, and Obesity Management. With years of hands-on experience, she is known for her precision, patient-first approach, and commitment to delivering safe and effective treatments.

She strongly believes in minimally invasive techniques, faster recovery, and holistic patient care. Her ability to combine surgical expertise with aesthetic understanding makes her a trusted name among patients seeking both medical and cosmetic solutions.

Qualifications:

MBBS, MS (General Surgery), PDCC (Cosmetology, Trichology & Obesity Management)

Dr. Varsha Kukreja

Cosmetology & Aesthetic Specialist
Skin Aesthetics | Trichology | Obesity Consultant

Dr. Varsha Kukreja specializes in Cosmetology, Skin Care, and Hair Treatments, focusing on enhancing natural beauty through safe and advanced procedures. She is passionate about helping patients regain confidence through personalized aesthetic solutions.

Her approach combines modern dermatological techniques with a deep understanding of patient needs, ensuring natural-looking and long-lasting results. She emphasizes patient education, transparency, and customized treatment plans for every individual.

Qualifications:

MBBS, MS, PDCC (Cosmetology, Trichology & Obesity Management)
