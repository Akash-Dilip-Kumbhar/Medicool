Dr. Benjamin Harris

Dr. Benjamin Harris is a highly respected and experienced medical professional specializing in cardiology. With over 15+ years of expertise in diagnosing and managing complex heart conditions, Dr. Harris is dedicated to providing personalized, patient-centered care. Beyond his clinical expertise, Dr. Harris is passionate about educating patients, empowering them to take charge of their health through informed decision-making. His philosophy of care is rooted in trust, open communication, and a deep understanding of each patient's unique needs.

Education And Training

A graduate of Harvard Medical School, Dr. Harris has earned recognition for his innovative treatment strategies and his commitment to staying at the forefront of medical advancements. He has participated in numerous research projects.

  • Doctor of Medicine (MD) from Harvard Medical School (2005)
  • Residency in Internal Medicine at Johns Hopkins (2010)
  • Fellowship in Cardiology, University of Oxford (2014)

Board Certification

Board Certified in Cardiovascular Disease by the American Board of Internal Medicine.

Professional Memberships

  • Fellow of the American College of Cardiology (FACC)
  • Member of the American Heart Association (AHA)
  • Member of the Society for Cardiovascular Angiography and Interventions (SCAI)
